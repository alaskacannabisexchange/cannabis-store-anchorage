About Our Dispensary

Alaska Cannabis Exchange is your trusted Anchorage destination for premium-quality cannabis. Situated at 2906 Spenard Rd, we foster a welcoming environment for both newcomers and aficionados.

Our passionate budtenders offer personalized education to help you select products tailored to wellness, relaxation, or enjoyment.

We rigorously curate our assortment, featuring top-tier flowers, edibles, concentrates, and accessories sourced from Alaska’s finest producers.
